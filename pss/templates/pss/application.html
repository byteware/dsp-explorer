Use this page to submit your application to the 2017 OpenMaker Pilot Support Scheme!
By funding the best 20 projects, we want to:

  • Foster collaboration between Makers and Manufacturers
  • Prototype innovations, be that through products, production processes, supply or value chains, distribution, or ownership
  • Encourage partnerships that are sustainable and deliver social impact

If you believe your proposal suits these objectives, then we hope you apply!

You can start by taking a look to the following documents

Download Application Form
Check Terms of Reference

Download the application form, save it on your pc and fill it in (yes, it is an editable pdf!). Don’t forget to upload it (using the “Upload PDF” bottom below) and you are done for now.
If you are preselected, we will request you to submit further documents (you can read the full list in the Terms of Reference).
At that point, you will have a couple of weeks to prepare, collect and submit all the documents, so we recommend you to familiarise with the documents as soon as possible.

Good luck for the submission!
